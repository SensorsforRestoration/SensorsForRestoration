How to post an update

  1. Open updates.html → click the ✏️ Edit button.
  2. Copy the block below (from <article class="card update"> to </article>).
  3. Paste it above the first update and fill in date, team, and details.
  4. Scroll down → Commit changes. The site updates automatically.
