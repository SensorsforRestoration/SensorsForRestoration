Context

Lake Bardawil is a shallow, hypersaline lagoon on the Sinai’s northern coast whose ecological health and fisheries have declined due to sedimentation and limited water exchange with the Mediterranean. Restoration plans focus on improving exchange and rebuilding a robust coastal water cycle. Our contribution is continuous, affordable water-quality monitoring to inform decisions over time.

Technical Tracks

  • Electro-conductivity for salinity; optical CV for nitrate/nitrite
  • Power: solar sizing, battery chemistry, duty cycling
  • Comms: LoRa, LTE, or relay; data formats & logging
  • Hull design: buoyancy, waterproofing, maintainability

Partner & Approach

Work aligns with broader Sinai restoration efforts and the need to scale proven interventions via automation in remote or hazardous settings. Students iterate quickly, prototype, and validate in local water bodies analogous to Bardawil before deployment.
